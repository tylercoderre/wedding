FAQ.

A few good to knows about the wedding.

If you have further questions, please reach out to us and we can add them for others to refer to.

What should I wear?

Semi-formal attire. We're not going super fancy, and the theme will be floral and wildflowers. Buffalo tends to still be pretty warm in September, but can get chilly at night. The ceremony and reception will be in the greenhouses, while drinks and games will be held outdoors (weather permitting).

- Tyler & Shana
What is the parking situation?

We have the entire venue reserved, so the parking lots there will be available to guests. There will be signage to help you navigate to where you're going.

- Tyler & Shana
Where should I stay?

If you'll be coming and staying in for the night, there are a variety of hotels, and AirBnBs around. We did not do a room block any any place in particular for guests outside of the wedding party so those attending can stay at the place that best suites your needs and price points. If you have questions or want some suggestions of places, then we'd love to help out.

- Tyler & Shana
